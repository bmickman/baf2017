2016

The first Bushwick Arts Festival occured in June 2016! The purpose of the event is to give both artists and art lovers a fun, June-based event to share art and network. Bushwick Arts Festival primarily aims to allow artists who are not represented by galleries the opportunity to show their art to the public by utlizing the intimate atmosphere of their private spaces and mingle with interested parties.

2017!

From the experience gained in the debut event, BAF hopes to achieve greater turnout than before by addressing the top issues of last year, mainly promotion and implementation. The new dates will decrease calendar conflict with other high profile June events in NYC. The higly requested printed maps, a more detailed online guide, and drastically increased signage will get people into studio spaces with greater ease. Other recommendations are welcome!
